Hypothesis:
Secreted proteins avoid small folded domains in their first 100 residues.
Approach
  • Generate list of all secreted proteins or all cytosolic/nuclear proteins in the human genome
  • Map domains in the first 100 residues using HMM sequence profiles generated for all known folds
    • Superfamily: SCOP
    • Gene3D: CATH
  • Calculate fraction of proteins that contain short (50 or 100 residues) domains within the first 100 residues

Molecular dynamics
  • Coordinates change according to velocity
  • Velocities change according to forces
  • Forces are calculated according to energy function
Monte-Carlo sampling
  • Structure is modified by random move
  • New structure is evaluated by energy function
  • If the move leads to worse energy it is reversed with a probability based on the energy increase
